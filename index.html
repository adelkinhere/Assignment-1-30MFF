Catching the Sun
on NYUAD Campus

About

Stepping outside after hours in an air-conditioned room, the warmth of the sun feels like an instant recharge. Since moving to Abu Dhabi, I've realized how deeply my mood and energy depend on the weather. The sun gives me the energy I need every day.

Believing in the sun's positive effects on mental well-being, I decided to capture its presence on campus through film.
